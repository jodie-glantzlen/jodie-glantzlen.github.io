My long term career pathway

I can think of two main options for me. Either work for a web-development or SAAS company, or work freelance on personal projects my partner and I have. It’s too early to say, because even though I’m enjoying this course so far and I like front-end development, I have no idea what being a web developer really involves (I have never done back-end, or database, or even languages other than JS, CSS and HTML). Maybe I’ll realise being in the heart of tech is not for me and I’ll turn to other options while staying in the industry.

Long story short, I don’t know! One step at a time is my motto.

My learning style: strengths and weaknesses

My strong empathetic nature helps me understand people’s needs and feelings. I’m good at always choosing my words carefully or behaving in a mindful way. This makes me a good team player. I’ve always thought learning and growing together as a group is more effective and powerful, which is the main reason why I enrolled in this course. I’ve tried twice to learn on my own and never succeeded. However, as an INFJ, I’m very sensitive and prone to stress and anxiety. This can be both a strength and weakness. I’ve used stress before as a really effective motivator. But in negative environments where I don’t feel supported or listened to, it sucks all my energy and motivation away and tends to freeze me. It all comes down again to empathy and finding the right fit!
